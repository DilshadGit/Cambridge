Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to use Osmo?

You just need an iPad version 2 or higher. iPad Mini works too.

What ages is Osmo designed for?

5 and up.

Can adults play the games too?

Absolutely! Osmo has been designed for 5 and up but many adults love playing the games. The later levels get very challenging.

What exactly will I get when I buy Osmo?

The Starter Kit comes with the following items: A reflective mirror for the iPad Camera; white iPad Stand; 2 sets of game pieces (2 complete alphabet letter tile sets and 7 wood tangram shapes) and instructions for downloading 4 free apps from the App Store.

The Genius Kit comes with everything above along with the Numbers game pieces (2 sets of number tiles and 2 sets of dots tiles) and instructions to download the Numbers game for free from the App Store.

Do kids like it?

Yep. Osmo has been tested in thousands of schools across the world and kids love it! They spend hours with the games. We’ve taken input and advice from scores of educators, parents and kids to ensure this.
